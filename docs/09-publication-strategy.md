## Ranking logic & sequencing

- **Sequencing:** publish #1 (field-founding) first to plant the flag → flagship empirical
  (#3/#4/#5) for policy traction → methods (#6/#10/#11) for citation durability → Tier 2 in
  parallel as flagship data matures.
- **Portfolio balance:** ~40% modelling/optimization, ~20% causal/observational, ~20% methods,
  ~10% simulation, ~10% review/protocol/perspective — a mix that builds both impact and a
  citable methodological core.
- **Every manuscript maps to a project (Part 2) and a research question (E03)** — no orphan papers.
