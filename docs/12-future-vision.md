# Part 12 — Future Vision

Where Public Health Decision Analytics goes once the Centre is established.

## 1. The 5-year vision: the learning health system, realized

Every state runs a **continuously re-optimized** health budget. Each quarter, new routine data
updates the burden posterior; the optimizer re-allocates; the digital twin stress-tests; tiered
briefs flow to decision-makers; impact evaluation measures realized DALYs averted and feeds back.
Allocation stops being an annual political ritual and becomes a **measured, improving, auditable
process**. The Allocation Inefficiency Index falls year over year — a publicly tracked number.

## 2. The 10-year vision: PHDA as standard practice

- **A named discipline** with its own curriculum, journal section, conference, and chairs.
- **A global commons:** a federated, privacy-preserving network of country PHDA platforms
  sharing methods and (governed) models — the decision-analytics analogue of GBD.
- **Causal foundation models for health systems**, trained on multi-country routine data, that
  give every district a calibrated digital twin out of the box.
- **One-Health integration:** human, animal, and environmental burden in one decision
  substrate, enabling cross-ministry optimal allocation (P50).

## 3. Scientific frontiers to open

- **Mechanism design for allocation** — incentive-compatible budget pooling across agencies so
  integrated optimization (P38) is politically self-enforcing.
- **Real-time / streaming decision analytics** — allocation that updates on weekly surveillance,
  not annual surveys.
- **Causal reinforcement learning** for safe sequential policy under the twin.
- **Equity formalization** — moving from weights to deliberatively-elicited, contestable social
  welfare functions with a binding deontic floor.
- **Planetary-health coupling** — climate, food, and One-Health drivers endogenous to the burden
  model.

## 4. The normative commitment that must survive scale

As PHDA gains power to move money, three guardrails are permanent:
1. **Equity floor + independent Ethics Board veto** — efficiency never overrides the worst-off.
2. **Honest uncertainty** — every recommendation ships its credible interval and assumptions.
3. **Auditability** — every decision traces to a reproducible run; recommendations are
   contestable in public. Legitimacy, not just accuracy, is the goal (Part 1 §1.4).

## 5. The measurable promise

PHDA's bet, stated as a falsifiable claim to be proven over a decade:

> *Health systems that adopt continuous decision analytics avert measurably more avoidable
> death, morbidity, and catastrophic expenditure — more equitably — than those that do not,
> at no additional cost.*

The first evidence already exists in this blueprint: a computed +41.7% in equity-weighted
DALYs averted at constant budget ([E09](engines/E09-optimization-engine.md)). The 10-year programme
exists to turn that single computed result into a global, demonstrated, institutionalized fact.

## 6. Closing

PHDAROP begins from a simple, uncomfortable premise — that much avoidable suffering is a
**decision** made elsewhere, with incomplete analytics — and ends by building the science,
software, and institutions to make those decisions better. The field is named. The methods are
specified and, where it matters most, already computed. The roadmap is short and starts now.
